makes 1 8½x4½" loaf | total time: 30 minutes

BANANA BREAD

ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1¼ tsp baking soda
  • ¾ tsp kosher salt
  • 1 cup (packed) dark brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup plain whole-milk greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup / ½ stick unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 2 large eggs (or substitute with chia egg)
  • 4 large very ripe bananas, mashed
  • ½ cup semisweet chocolate chips (optional)

method

  • preheat oven to 350°, lightly coat loaf pan oil & line with parchment paper, leaving a generous overhang on long sides
  • whisk flour, baking soda, & salt in a medium bowl
  • use a whisk/hand mixer (i just use a whisk for a little nit of arm workout), beat brown sugar, yogurt, & butter in a large bowl until light & fluffy
  • add eggs one at a time, beating to blend after each addition & scraping down sides & bottom of bowl as needed
  • add flour mixture slowly & mix until just combined
  • add bananas & mix until just combined
  • fold in chocolate chips, if using
  • pour & scrape batter into pan & smooth top
  • bake for 60-65 minutes
  • transfer pan to a wire rack & let bread cool completely (if you can resist) before slicing
